Persimmon and Endive Salad

Time to make: 15 minutes

Yield: 4

Description

Start your meal with a refreshing salad made with endives, persimmons, pomegranate seeds, and avocados, drizzled with honey vinegar and avocado oil vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of honey vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup of unrefined avocado oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Pepper
  • 4 heads of Belgian endive, leaves seperated
  • 2 persimmons, very thinly sliced crosswise on a mandoline
  • 1 Hass avocadoâ€”peeled, pitted and cut into thin wedges
  • 1/2 cup of toasted almonds, chopped, plus more for garnish
  • 1/4 of cup pomegranate seeds, plus more for garnish

Recipe Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the vinegar with Dijon.
    2.
  2. While whisking constantly, slowly drizzle in the avocado oil until incorporated; season with salt and pepper.
    3.
  3. Gently fold in the remaining ingredients and season with salt and pepper.
    4.
  4. Garnish with additional almonds and pomegranate seeds and serve.
